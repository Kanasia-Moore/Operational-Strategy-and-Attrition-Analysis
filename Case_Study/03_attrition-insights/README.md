# Attrition Insights

This section connects workforce structure and employee voice findings to identify potential drivers of attrition within fulfillment center environments.

## Purpose

Synthesize operational observations and employee sentiment into broader workforce insights.

## Case Study Focus

- Root cause analysis
- Management instability
- Workload design
- Burnout and turnover patterns

## Core Insight

"The system is optimized for output — not people."

The analysis suggests that operational efficiency is prioritized heavily, while recovery systems, managerial consistency, and employee support structures may be insufficient to sustain long-term workforce stability.
