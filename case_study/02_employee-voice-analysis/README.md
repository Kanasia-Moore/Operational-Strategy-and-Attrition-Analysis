# Employee Voice Analysis

This section focuses on employee sentiment, recurring themes, and operational concerns identified through voice data analysis.

## Purpose

Analyze how employees describe their workplace experiences and identify patterns connected to burnout and attrition.

## Case Study Focus

- Burnout and fatigue
- Poor communication and support
- Emotional detachment from work
- Sentiment analysis findings

## Key Insight

Employee voice data consistently points toward exhaustion, operational pressure, and lack of support as recurring workplace themes.
