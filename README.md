# Operational Strategy & Attrition Analysis (Externship Case Study)

## Overview
This repository documents my work throughout an Amazon externship focused on operational strategy and workforce attrition.

The goal of this project is to explore how operational design, workforce structure, and leadership practices influence employee experience and retention within fulfillment environments.

Rather than presenting only final outputs, this repository captures the full analytical process — including weekly projects, key questions, reflections, and evolving insights.

---

## Project Structure

### Weekly Projects
Each week is structured as its own mini case study, including:
- objectives and deliverables  
- key analytical questions  
- insights and findings  
- supporting data, code, and visuals  

### Reflections
Alongside each project, I document reflections on what I learned, how my thinking evolved, and areas for improvement.

### Final Case Study (In Progress)
The final phase of this project will synthesize all findings into a case study focused on identifying and analyzing the drivers of attrition.

---

## Current Focus Areas

- Workforce structure and operational workflow  
- Employee voice and sentiment analysis  
- The role of management in employee experience  
- How operational pressure flows across different roles  

---

## Key Direction

A central theme emerging from this project is the relationship between:

Operational Design + Management Support → Employee Experience → Attrition

This repository is an ongoing exploration of that relationship.
